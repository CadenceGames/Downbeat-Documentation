# Editor Controls 
## Camera Movement 

|Combination| Action |
|:---:|:---|
|`Right click + W` | Move Camerea Forward|
|`Right click + S` | Move Camera Backwards|
|`Right click + A` | Move Camera Left|
|`Right click + D` | Move Camera Right|
|`Right click + E` | Move Camera Up|
|`Right click + Q` | Move Camera Down|
|`Right click + Mouse movement` | Pan Camera View|
|`Right click + Left Shift` | Increase Movement Speed |
|`Right click + H` | Teleport Camera to Starting Position|

## Editor Shortcuts

|Combination|Action|
|:---:|:---|
|`Space` or `K`|Play/Pause|
|`J`|Move time back one tick|
|`L`|Move time forwards one tick|
|---|---|
|`CTRL + F11`|Show/Hide Editor UI|
|`CTRL + 1`|Switch to Preview Screen|
|`CTRL + 2`|Switch to Path Editor Screen|
|---|---|
|`CTRL + N`|Create New Song|
|`CTRL + O`|Load Song|
|`CTRL + R`|Reload Song|
|`CTRL + S`|Save Song|
|---|---|
|`CTRL + Z`|Undo|
|`CTRL + Y`|Redo|
|`CTRL + X`|Cut|
|`CTRL + C`|Copy|
|`CTRL + V`|Paste|
|`Delete`|Delete|
